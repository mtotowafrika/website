Who we are

Mtoto W’afrika Child Care Ministries was founded in 2013 and fully incorporated with an NGO status in 2014. Our path towards transformation is hinged on the willingness and hope to spread the unconditional love of Jesus Christ to the next generation of leaders in the hard-to-reach communities in Uganda and across borders. This has been the greatest fuel behind the beautiful journey of the organization. The dream to actualize this leader is achieved through empowering this child through all the phases of growth. The heart of this started through the “SANYUKA” program in Luwero District, where the long life impact was to put those big smiles on these children.This has grown overtime into a full program under the core thematic area of education.

Our founder (Mr. Samuel Sekibejja) has lived this reality having been groomed by people of good will who were compelled to give a ray of hope to the less privileged. Together with a wonderful team whose minds were aligned in the same direction, Mtoto W’Afrika was born to continue shading a ray of hope to such people and this is a vision that we continue to pursue for life.
