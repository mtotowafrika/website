OUR PROGRAMS

See some pictures of our previous events and outreaches.

EDUCATION

  • Child sponsorship - We pay school fees for children, and we give them scholastic support.
  • Teachers’ Empowerment Program: We organize teachers’ training workshops for teachers in school.

SKILLS & TALENTS

  • We work with schools and communities to offer their children career guidance.
  • We teach children computer & other ICT skills in their communities.
  • We also teach adolescents in communities tailoring skills.

COMMUNITY HEALTH CARE

  • We offer both individual & community medical support, through medical camps , general health education, and donations (we give sanitary pads to girls in schools).

Our Causes

We run a variety of programs covering various SDGs in order to achieve our vision and goals for the African Children.
